How it works

Four steps.
No money involved.

Anyone can join a group. But you can only join this one by completing a real exchange with a real person first.

1
Tell us what you offer and what you need

Not a profile. Two simple things: what you're genuinely ready to give — your time, your skill, your space, your hands — and what you're genuinely looking for in return.

2
We find you one person

Not a list. Not a marketplace. One person whose offer matches your need, and whose need matches your offer. You don't browse. You receive a match.

3
You exchange

Both of you give. Both of you receive. After it happens, you each confirm it was real. That's it. No rating. No review. Just: did it happen?

4
Now you're inside

The community is made up only of people who have already exchanged. You can see what others offer and need. You can exchange again and again. Your place here is earned — and it grows.
